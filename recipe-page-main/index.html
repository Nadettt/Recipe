Vanilla Cupcake Recipe

Bake these easy vanilla cupcakes in just 35 minutes. Perfect for birthdays, picnics or whenever you fancy a sweet treat, they're sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 35 minutes
  • Preparation: 20 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 110g softened butter
  • 80g golden caster sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 110g self-raising flour

For the buttercream

  • 100g softened butter
  • 200g icing sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • food colouring paste of your choice (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 180C/160C fan and fill a 12 cupcake tray with cases.
  2. Whisk: Using an electric whisk beat 110g softened butter and 80g golden caster sugar together until pale and fluffy then whisk in 2 large eggs, one at a time, scraping down the sides of the bowl after each addition.
  3. Add ½ tsp vanilla extract, 110g self-raising flour and a pinch of salt, whisk until just combined then spoon the mixture into the cupcake cases.
  4. Bake for 15 mins until golden brown and a skewer inserted into the middle of each cake comes out clean. Leave to cool completely on a wire rack.
  5. Buttercream: To make the buttercream, whisk 100g softened butter until super soft then add 200g icing sugar, 1 tsp vanilla extract and a pinch of salt.
  6. Whisk together until smooth (start off slowly to avoid an icing sugar cloud) then beat in 3 tbsp milk.
  7. Colour: If wanting to colour, stir in the food colouring now. Spoon or pipe onto the cooled cupcakes.
